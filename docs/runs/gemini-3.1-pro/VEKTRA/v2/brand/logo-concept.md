# VEKTRA Logo Concept v2

## Concept
The upgraded VEKTRA logo moves beyond a literal digital bar graph to a sophisticated expression of signal processing. It features three nested "V" geometries, perfectly aligned. This arrangement evokes interference patterns, geometric echoes, and an oscilloscope's Lissajous curves.

## Geometry & Mark
The mark consists of three V-shaped strokes with decreasing opacity (100%, 60%, 20%) and identical angles. This creates a tunneling, three-dimensional effect of a signal propagating forward or an echo decaying in time.

## Logotype
The logotype utilizes JetBrains Mono with generous tracking (+0.15em) to enforce technical precision. The juxtaposition of the sharp, dynamic mark and the monospaced, rigid text encapsulates the "hacker precision vs. expressive motion" aesthetic perfectly.

## Animation Behavior
The entrance animation uses an SVG path drawing technique (`stroke-dashoffset`). The outermost V traces itself first, followed by the inner layers in quick succession. This simulates a high-speed laser rendering the logo onto a screen. Once the mark resolves, the logotype softly translates and fades in.
