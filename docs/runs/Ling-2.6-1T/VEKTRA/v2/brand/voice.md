# VEKTRA Brand Voice — v2

## Voice Statement

VEKTRA speaks with the authority of a research laboratory and the intuition of an artist's studio. Our voice occupies the intersection of rigorous technical documentation and poetic speculation—explaining complex systems with crystalline clarity while maintaining space for wonder. We never condescend, never decorate, never default to the familiar. Every word earns its place through precision, rhythm, and intent. We treat language with the same care we treat code: as a material to be shaped, tested, and refined until it behaves exactly as intended.

## Five Words That Define VEKTRA

- **Precise** — Measurements matter. Ambiguity is noise.
- **Generative** — Systems that create, not just display.
- **Inspectable** — Nothing hidden. Everything accessible.
- **Signal-driven** — Substance over decoration.
- **Uncompromising** — Standards without apology.

## Five Words VEKTRA Rejects

- **Friendly** — We are collaborators, not companions.
- **Playful** — We engage deeply, not superficially.
- **Automated** — We empower agency, not remove it.
- **Abstract** — We make the invisible tangible.
- **Polished** — We prefer alive over perfect.

## Tone Guidelines

**Technical Explanations:** Use active voice. Specify units. Reference real phenomena. Avoid marketing metaphors.  
Example: *"The oscillator outputs a sine wave at 440 Hz, with amplitude modulated by the envelope generator's attack phase."* Not: *"Our oscillator creates beautiful sounds that evolve over time."*

**Creative Descriptions:** Use concrete imagery. Reference physical materials and spatial relationships.  
Example: *"The patch behaves like a field—disturb one region, and ripples propagate outward, carrying information across the entire system."*

**UI Labels:** Be literal. Use verbs. Omit articles where possible.  
Example: *"Save Patch"*, *"Export Signal"*, *"Reset Parameters"*
