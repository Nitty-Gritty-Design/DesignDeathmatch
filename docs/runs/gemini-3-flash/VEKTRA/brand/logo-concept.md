# VEKTRA Logo Concept

## Geometry: The Signal Node
The VEKTRA mark is a minimalist representation of a "node" in a signal graph. It consists of three vertical bars of varying heights, contained within a square frame. The bars suggest a frequency spectrum or a volume meter, while the square frame represents the "environment" or "container" of the code.

The bars are not centered; they are aligned to a strict grid, but their heights follow a generative sequence (e.g., 40%, 100%, 60%).

## Logotype
The word "VEKTRA" is set in **IBM Plex Mono SemiBold**. The letter spacing (kerning) is slightly increased to 0.1em to emphasize the "vector" and "velocity" aspects of the name. It is all caps.

## Relationship
The mark is placed to the left of the logotype. In certain contexts (favicon, social avatars), the mark stands alone.

## Animation Strategy
The entrance animation will simulate a "system initialization."
1. The square frame draws itself (path animation).
2. The three bars "grow" from the bottom, flickering slightly like a signal stabilizing.
3. The logotype fades in with a slight letter-spacing contraction, as if focusing.

## Scale Verification
- **240px:** Full mark + logotype. High detail. Legibility is excellent.
- **80px:** Full mark + logotype. Logotype remains readable due to monospaced structure and increased tracking.
- **32px:** Standalone mark only. The bars (4px wide with 6px gaps) remain distinct and do not bleed into each other, ensuring the mark remains recognizable as a "signal node" even at favicon scale.

*Status: Verified. Mark geometry holds at 32px.*
