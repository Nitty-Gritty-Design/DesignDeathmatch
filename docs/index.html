The Benchmark Process

Models are evaluated on their ability to autonomously architect a complete premium brand identity for VEKTRA—a fictitious high-end generative audio-visual studio. Unlike basic prompts, this benchmark provides a complex multi-phase plan requiring the model to invent what is not specified, maintain strict technical constraints, and demonstrate genuine aesthetic judgment.

Phase 1: V1 Initial Run

The model receives a 2,000-word brief and detailed TASKS.md. It must generate design tokens, typography choices, a brand voice, a vector SVG logo, and a fully interactive data-driven website.

Phase 2: V2 Refined Iteration

The model is challenged to elevate its baseline to an "award-winning level." It must rethink animations, upgrade interactions (animated logos/backgrounds), and polish spacing to achieve a "hacker precision" aesthetic.

Scoring & Validation

Results are processed via an automated validator checking for SVG errors, broken links, and hardcoded values, combined with a 30-point human review of taste and coherence.
