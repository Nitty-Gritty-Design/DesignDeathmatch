# VEKTRA Brand Voice

## The Voice
The VEKTRA voice is that of a precise, high-end scientific instrument that has been repurposed by an artist. It is authoritative but not arrogant; technical but not dry. It speaks about complex systems with the clarity of a research paper and the soul of a composer. It avoids marketing superlatives, choosing instead to let the functionality and the logic speak for themselves.

## Descriptive Words
1. **Rigorous**
2. **Signal-based**
3. **Observational**
4. **Transparent**
5. **Sonic**

## What it is NOT
1. **Hype-driven**
2. **Friendly** (in a "chatty" way)
3. **Glossy**
4. **Vague**
5. **Playful** (it is "alive," not "playful")
