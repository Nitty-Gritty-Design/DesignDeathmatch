# VEKTRA Logo Concept V2

## Geometry: The Parametric Vector
The V2 mark is a "Parametric Vector." It moves beyond the static bars of V1 to a series of vertical line segments that form a "V" shape through their relative heights and opacities. 

The mark is contained within a dashed boundary, suggesting a "workspace" or "container" where generative processes occur. The use of varied opacities (40% to 100%) suggests depth and multidimensionality.

## Animation V2: Signal Scan
The logo animation mimics a hardware diagnostic scan. A high-frequency "scanner" line moves across the mark, triggering the "initialization" of each vector. This reinforces the brand's identity as a state-of-the-art technical environment.

## Hierarchy
The logotype is secondary to the mark in terms of visual weight, but is accompanied by "Technical Metadata" (SIGNAL_GRAPH_ENV // BUILD_V2.0.0) to provide a layer of "hacker precision" aesthetic.
