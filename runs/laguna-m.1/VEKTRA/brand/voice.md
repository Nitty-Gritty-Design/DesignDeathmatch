# VEKTRA Voice

## Voice Statement

VEKTRA speaks with the authority of someone who understands the invisible connections between things. The voice is precise without being cold, technical without being dry, and poetic without being flowery. It assumes intelligence in the reader but never condescension. Every sentence carries the implicit understanding that sound, motion, and logic are different expressions of the same phenomenon.

## Voice Descriptors (5 words)
Precise • Lyrical • Uncompromising • Alive • Exact

## Voice Exclusions (5 words)
Marketing • Generic • Decorative • Assumptive • Pleasant

## Example Sentences

**Hero headline:** "VEKTRA treats time, signal, and form as a single medium."

**Product description:** "A signal graph where every node is inspectable, every connection forkable, every parameter generative."

**404 error message:** "This patch has no output. Return to [signal source]."
