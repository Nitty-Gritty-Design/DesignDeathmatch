# VEKTRA Logo Concept

## Geometry of the Mark

The VEKTRA mark is a 2x2 grid forming a quadrant with a central intersection. Each quadrant contains a different geometric element:
- Top-left: A sharp angle suggesting a rising waveform peak
- Top-right: A descending vector implying frequency modulation  
- Bottom-left: A horizontal bar representing a sustained signal
- Bottom-right: An empty space that creates tension and allows the form to breathe

The mark is constructed from straight lines at 45-degree angles, suggesting digital precision while avoiding the literalism of oscilloscope waves. The negative space between elements creates implied motion — the eye travels around the mark like a signal through a patch.

## Logotype Treatment

The word "VEKTRA" is set in Inter Bold with slightly condensed tracking. The K is subtly modified — the diagonal stroke extends slightly to create visual weight balance. The overall weight feels substantial enough for terminal darkness without appearing heavy.

## Relationship Between Mark and Logotype

The mark sits to the left of the logotype in a horizontal lockup. The mark's height equals the cap height of the logotype, creating a consistent vertical rhythm. The mark can also function independently at small sizes due to its geometric simplicity.

## Animation Concept

The animated logo reveals in sequence: the four quadrants fade in with a 50ms stagger, then the intersection point pulses once with the accent color. Total duration: 2.2 seconds. The animation feels like a system achieving signal lock — precise, earned, alive.
