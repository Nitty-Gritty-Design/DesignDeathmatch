# VEKTRA Typography

## Monospace: JetBrains Mono

JetBrains Mono is chosen for VEKTRA because it carries the precision of terminal typography while being uniquely beautiful at display sizes. Its slightly condensed character counters and clear distinction between similar characters (0/O, l/1/I) make it ideal for code-as-content and for when the logo mark appears large. The open-source nature aligns with VEKTRA''s transparency ethos, and the italic forms have an unexpected geometric quality that works well in technical documentation.

- Import: `@import url(''https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=JetBrains+Mono:ital,wght@0,100;0,200;0,300;0,400;0,500;0,600;0,700;0,800;1,100;1,200;1,300;1,400;1,500;1,600;1,700;1,800&display=swap'');`

## Sans-serif: Inter

Inter is chosen for VEKTRA because it exists at the intersection of technical precision and warmth. Its large x-height maintains readability at small sizes (11px labels) while its varied weight spectrum supports editorial hierarchy. The neutral geometric forms don''t compete with the monospace''s technical authority, but its subtle humanist details prevent the combination from feeling sterile. For a brand that treats code as poetry, Inter provides the rhythm section.

- Import: `@import url(''https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Inter:ital,opsz,wght@0,14..32,100..900;1,14..32,100..900&display=swap'');`
