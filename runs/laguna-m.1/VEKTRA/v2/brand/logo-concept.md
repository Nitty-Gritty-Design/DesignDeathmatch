# VEKTRA Logo Concept v2

## Geometry of the Mark

The VEKTRA mark is an octahedral signal vector — two interlocking triangles forming a precise geometric relationship. The design suggests:

- **Top triangle:** Frequency domain (pointing up)
- **Bottom triangle:** Time domain (pointing down, inverted)
- **Intersection:** The point where audio becomes visual, signal becomes form

The mark is constructed from three connected segments at 60-degree angles, creating a sense of motion and oscillation. The negative space between the triangles creates a dynamic tension — the "breathing room" where signals resonate.

## Logotype Treatment

The word "VEKTRA" is set in a custom tracking with distinctive alternation between wide and tight characters. The K is vertically asymmetric — the left leg extends subtly to create a waveform peak. The R's leg crosses the bowl at a deliberate angle, suggesting signal modulation.

The weight is medium (500) — not too thin to disappear, not too bold to dominate. The height ratio to the mark is precisely 1.2:1.

## Relationship Between Mark and Logotype

The mark sits to the left with a 0.75x gap (visually tight). The mark's apex aligns with the logotype's cap height, creating a unified vertical axis. At small sizes, the mark alone is recognizable through its triangular negative space.

## Animation Concept

The animated logo performs a spatial transformation:
1. Start: Two triangles separated, rotated 180° from final position
2. 0.3s: Triangles rotate and translate toward center
3. 0.6s: Intersection point flares with accent color
4. 1.2s: Subtle breathing pulse (scale 1.0 → 1.03 → 1.0)

Total duration: 1.8 seconds. The animation feels like a system achieving phase lock.

## Size Verification

- 240px: Perfect clarity, all geometric relationships visible
- 80px: Mark holds, logotype remains legible
- 32px: Mark only — the negative space is the defining feature
