06 — Voice

Brand voice

VEKTRA speaks like a system that knows exactly what it is. Not a marketer. Not a tutorial. Not a manifesto. The voice is the voice of a tool that has been used enough to develop opinions — precise, confident, occasionally poetic, never decorative. It treats the reader as a peer: someone who already thinks in signals and parameters, or is ready to. It explains without condensing. It describes without selling. Every sentence should feel like it was written by the same person who designed the product — someone who cares about the difference between frequency and pitch, between a waveform and a sound, between a tool and a language.

The voice is:
Precise Electric Technical Poetic Berlin
The voice is not:
Hypeful Cute Corporate Obtuse Generic
