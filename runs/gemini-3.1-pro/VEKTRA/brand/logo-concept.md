# VEKTRA Logo Concept

## Concept
The VEKTRA logo is composed of three precise vertical strokes of varying heights and widths, suggesting a digital waveform, an oscilloscope reading, or a minimalist "V". 

## Geometry & Mark
The mark is constructed on a strict geometric grid, using sharp 90-degree angles to enforce the "brutalist architect" feel. There are no curves. It is a literal representation of a discrete digital signal.

## Logotype
The mark is paired with a logotype set in JetBrains Mono, fully capitalized, with slight tracking applied to emphasize spacing and technical precision. The spacing connects the mark to the terminal origins of the tool.

## Animation Behavior
In the animated entrance, the three bars do not simply fade in; they scale vertically from a central zero-crossing line, mimicking a signal abruptly waking up. They stabilize into their final forms over 1.5 seconds with a custom cubic-bezier easing (`0.16, 1, 0.3, 1`), accompanied by a sudden opacity snap that feels like a monitor turning on.

## Scalability Check
- **240px (Desktop Header):** The full mark and logotype sit comfortably together, perfectly balanced.
- **80px (Mobile Nav):** The text remains legible, and the three bars of the mark retain their distinct height differences.
- **32px (Favicon):** The mark is used standalone (without the logotype). The three solid bars are thick enough to remain distinct and visually recognizable even at 16x16px or 32x32px.
