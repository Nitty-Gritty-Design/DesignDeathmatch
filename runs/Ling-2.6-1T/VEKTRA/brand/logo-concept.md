# VEKTRA Logo Concept

## Geometry of the Mark

The VEKTRA mark is a minimal waveform vector that suggests both signal propagation and geometric precision. It consists of three connected nodes forming an asymmetric peak — resembling a single cycle of a triangle wave or a simplified vector scope trace. The leftmost node anchors the mark, while the rightmost node extends upward and outward, suggesting velocity and direction. The stroke weight is consistent at 2 units, creating a technical drawing aesthetic rather than an organic shape.

The mark derives from the letter "V" but abstracts it beyond recognition — the negative space between the peak and baseline forms a subtle arrow pointing rightward, implying forward motion and signal flow. When rotated 90° clockwise, the mark resembles a simplified frequency spectrum with a single dominant peak.

## Logotype Treatment

The logotype uses IBM Plex Mono in Medium weight, set in uppercase with tight letter-spacing (tracking: 0.05em). The word "VEKTRA" sits to the right of the mark, aligned on the baseline. The monospace treatment reinforces the brand's technical foundation while the generous spacing creates an airy, deliberate rhythm. In the animated version, each letter appears sequentially with a 40ms stagger, simulating a terminal typing effect or signal propagation across a wire.

## Relationship Between Mark and Logotype

The mark functions as a standalone signal identifier — used at 32px and below, it appears without the logotype. At 80px and above, the logotype is always present. The mark's height matches the cap-height of the logotype (approximately 60% of the logotype's total height), creating visual harmony between the geometric symbol and the typographic system. The 2-unit stroke weight of the mark corresponds to the weight of the monospace font's vertical stems, establishing a proportional relationship between symbol and type.

## Animation Concept

The logo animation visualizes signal initialization — a system powering on and establishing a stable frequency. The mark draws itself stroke-by-stroke over 800ms using a custom ease-out-expo curve, suggesting a signal resolving from noise into clarity. Simultaneously, the logotype letters stagger in from left to right with a 40ms delay between each character, mimicking data transmission. A subtle glow (using the accent color at 20% opacity) pulses once at the mark's peak, indicating the "live" state. Total duration: 2.4 seconds. The animation runs once and holds on the final state — never looping, suggesting a system that is now active and stable.

## Size Verification

**240px width (desktop header):** Full mark + logotype with generous spacing. The mark is clearly visible as a signal icon.

**80px width (mobile nav):** Mark + logotype scaled proportionally. At this size, the monospace remains legible, and the mark retains its geometric clarity.

**32px width (favicon context):** Mark only. The three-node waveform remains distinguishable — the peak and baseline are clear at 32×32px. The 2-unit stroke weight maintains visibility, and the asymmetric shape prevents confusion with simpler geometric forms. No logotype.

**Verification:** The mark holds at all three sizes. At 32px, the waveform silhouette is immediately recognizable as a signal trace rather than a simple chevron or triangle, due to the extended baseline and the specific node placement (peak at 60% of width, creating a distinctive profile).
